# AI Assistant

Never miss an opportunity to book a customer. Our AI Assistant helps your business respond instantly, stay organized, and turn more conversations into confirmed appointments.

---

### Appointment Scheduling for Incoming Calls

When new callers reach your business, the AI Assistant can answer right away, collect the details you need, check availability, and guide the customer to a confirmed appointment. Your team stays focused while routine scheduling is handled quickly and consistently.

### Voicemail Callbacks That Recover Lost Leads

If a customer leaves a voicemail, the AI Assistant can call back promptly, continue the conversation, and help book a time that works. This follow-up flow helps reduce missed opportunities and keeps potential customers from moving on to a competitor.

---

### What This Helps You Achieve

* Fewer missed calls and faster response times
* More booked appointments from both live calls and voicemails
* Better customer experience with friendly, consistent communication
* More time for your team to focus on high-value work

### Typical Scheduling Flow

* Schedule appointments
* Check availability
* Confirm booking
* Send reminders
