## 🔒 Drupal Flat-Rate Upgrade Service
Keep your Drupal environments up-to-date, secure, and stable without the stress of emergency patching.

If you are running Drupal in production, updates are not optional. Core and contributed modules receive security releases and bug fixes regularly, and delaying updates increases risk over time. This service gives you a predictable monthly maintenance plan so your team can stay focused on business priorities while updates are handled professionally.

![Drupal upgrade planning and execution](/assets/images/upgrade-drupal.jpg){: .img-fluid style="max-height:720px; height:auto;" }

### What This Service Solves

Most teams do not struggle because they do not care about maintenance, they struggle because maintenance competes with feature deadlines, support requests, and growth initiatives. Over time, that creates a dangerous pattern: updates are postponed, vulnerabilities stay open longer than they should, and changes get bundled into high-risk maintenance windows. This service is designed to break that cycle with a routine process that keeps your platform current and reduces operational stress.

### What You Get Every Month

Each month, per environment, updates are completed using a repeatable workflow that prioritizes safety first and speed second. Before any update begins, backups are created for both code and database so there is always a known-good recovery point. Updates are then performed through composer to keep dependency management clean and auditable, followed by required database updates and configuration export to maintain consistency across environments. Finally, functional testing is completed to verify that critical site behavior still performs as expected after the update cycle.

### Feature Details

#### 1) Backup and Recovery Safety Net
Before changes are made, code and database backups are created so there is a clear rollback path if needed. This protects your environment from the most common maintenance risk: discovering an issue only after update steps have already modified live data.

#### 2) Composer-Based Update Workflow
Drupal core and contributed packages are updated using composer-driven best practices for consistency and traceability. This approach helps eliminate ad hoc update habits and gives your team a cleaner long-term dependency history.

#### 3) Database and Configuration Alignment
Database updates are applied and configuration is exported to help keep environments synchronized and predictable. When configuration stays aligned, deployment surprises are reduced and release quality improves over time.

#### 4) Functional Verification
Post-update validation checks core site functionality so update quality is reviewed before sign-off. Instead of assuming success because commands finished, this step confirms your site still behaves correctly where it matters most.

### Benefits for Your Business

The business value of this service is consistency. Routine upgrades lower security exposure, reduce the need for emergency after-hours interventions, and improve confidence before launches, marketing campaigns, or high-traffic periods. Over several months, clients usually see fewer surprise incidents, steadier platform behavior, and less developer time lost to maintenance interruptions. Instead of treating updates as a recurring fire drill, your organization gets a predictable operating rhythm with a fixed monthly cost.

### Expected Results

Clients typically choose this service to establish a dependable update cadence and remove uncertainty from maintenance planning. As this cadence matures, release workflows become cleaner, issue isolation becomes faster when changes do introduce defects, and teams gain stronger audit confidence through repeatable maintenance records. A consistent monthly process also helps slow the accumulation of technical debt in Drupal core and contributed dependencies.

### Ideal For

This service is a strong fit for organizations running one or more Drupal environments, especially when internal teams are already fully allocated to product and content work. It is also well suited for businesses that need predictable maintenance budgeting and for site owners preparing for growth, compliance requirements, or higher seasonal traffic.
